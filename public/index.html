Competition Details

The competition involves a sophisticated surface which our robot must navigate through and rescue ewoks along the way.

Chewbacca and the Ewoks are hiding from the Stormtroopers. Our challenge is to design an autonomous robot to rescue them. To win the competition, we need to maximize the points earned in each rescue mission.
